FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) - Team 188

Project Goal

Design and build a robot to compete in the annual FIRST Robotics Competition.

Project Overview

Each year I work with about 30 students from FRC team 188 to discuss what the robot should be capable of doing, design prototypes using SolidWorks, manufacture parts, assemble prototypes, and test the prototypes to verify they meet design requirements. Once the prototyping phase is finished, the final design will be created in SolidWorks, parts will be manufactured, and the robot will be assembled, wired, and programmed to compete. The robot will face off against 80+ teams across Ontario trying to secure a position at the World Championships where the team will compete against 400+ teams from across the world.

My Reponsibilities

Mechanical Mentor

  • Work with students to design prototypes using SolidWorks and build the prototypes using wood, sheet metal, and box tubing to test concepts.
  • Manufacture parts using simple machine shop tools and the in house CNC machine as per drawing and within acceptable tolerances
  • Oversee final assembly of the robot and help troubleshoot mechanical problems
