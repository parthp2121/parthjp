PCA Pump Design

Project Goal

Design a 3D model of a PCA pump that can be 3D printed.

Project Overview

I designed a 3D model of a PCA pump which had similar dimensions to the real pump. I designed it so that it could easily be 3D printed with minimal post processing required by the end user. I also created another piece to attach the 3D printed pump onto IV poles making sure all the hardware was easily accessible from local hardware stores.


Durham College's faculty member that requested this design was extremely happy with the final design because of the close geometrical resemble to the final design, minimal parts, and easy to assemble design. The facult member uses the 3D printed model in their nursing labs and has saved them thousands of dollars in equipment cost .
