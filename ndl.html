National Design League - Wireless Charger in Arm-rest

Project Goal

Design a wireless charger that can be incorporated into the rear passenger armrest.

Project Overview

My team (3 other members) and I designed a lid with a built-in wireless charger which would cover the cupholders in rear passenger armrest. When the lid is opened, there is space for two phones to be charged at the same time. The final design was created within 24 hours and was selected as the best design by the judges.

Design Features:

  • Design for Manufacturing/Design for Assembly - The final design used off the shelf fasteners and the parts would be easily manufactured and assembled.
  • Position Hold - 2 sets of magnets help keep the lid in place when it is closed or opened so the lid does not move while the car is in motion
  • The design included room for wiring so that all cabling was hidden from the user and there were no pinch points for the cable when opening on closing the lid
