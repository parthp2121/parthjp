Maze Solving Robot

Project Goal

Design and build a robot that can navigate a two-level maze, avoid obstacles, and identify a miner.

Project Overview

The team and I built a 4" x 4" x 6.5" robot which was capable of navigating a two-level maze using the shortest path possible. The robot was equipped with 4 IR sensors, 1 ultrasonic sensor, and 2 encoders to help correct its movement as it navigated the maze. Our team's robot was top 3 (tied) out of 11 teams.

My Reponsibilities

Team Lead

  • Designed the robot base and structure using NX which were 3D printed. All parts were designed with appropriate tolerances so that they can be press-fit together. Press fitting parts made it easier to test different designs quickly.
  • The robot design focused on Design for Manufacturing and Design for Assembly.
  • Created test plans to validate concept designs.
  • Packaged a microcontroller, motor shield, voltage regulator, power module, 4 IR sensors, an ultrasonic sensor, 2 encoders, 2 battery packs, 2 motors, a claw, and 2 servos within a 4” x 4” x 6.5” robot
