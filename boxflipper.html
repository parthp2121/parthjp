Box Flipper

Project Goal

Design a flipping mechanism to flip every second box coming on a conveyor and integrate a PLC to automate the tasks.

Project Overview

The team and I designed a flipping mechanism which flipped every other box 90 degrees two times to achieve the 180 degree rotation requirement. Splitting the task into two smaller tasks allowed the final design to be simpler, more efficient, and more versatile. Our design was only of the only designs that could be implemented on existing conveyor systems without modifying the conveyor. We also developed the Ladder Logic to automate the process.

My Reponsibilities

Design Lead

  • The final design was made with Design for Manufacturing and Design for Assembly in mind.
